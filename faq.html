Donations

Yes. RSMC is a registered non-profit in Pakistan, and donations qualify for tax exemption under Section 61 of the Income Tax Ordinance, 2001. Tax receipts are emailed within 7 business days. International donors should consult their local rules.

Yes. Our food, healthcare and education programs qualify under multiple Zakat heads (such as al-fuqara and al-masakin). Select "Zakat" in the designation field when donating and we'll allocate your funds to fully Zakat-eligible activities.

91% goes directly to program delivery — food, medicine, books, training. The remaining 9% covers operations: salaries for our small core team, audits, transport and basic office costs. Every figure is published in our annual audited report.

Credit/debit cards (VISA, Mastercard, AMEX), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, JazzCash, EasyPaisa, SadaPay, and direct bank transfer in PKR or USD. For corporate sponsorships, we also accept demand drafts.

Absolutely — at any time. Just email donations@rsmc.org.pk or call our donor line at +92 42 3588 0000. We'll cancel within one business day and confirm by email.

Programs & Beneficiaries

We work through trusted local community leaders and verified household assessments. Every beneficiary is registered with photo ID, family details and a needs assessment. We prioritise the poorest 20% of households in our service areas.

Currently Punjab (Bhakkar, Multan, Bahawalpur and surrounding rural districts) and southern Sindh, with a focus on Cholistan and remote villages. We expand based on capacity and donor commitments.

Please reach our beneficiary support line at +92 42 3588 0001 or email support@rsmc.org.pk. We'll guide you through the registration process. Note: we operate on limited capacity and prioritize the most vulnerable cases.

Volunteering & Partnership

Submit the form on our Get Involved page. We'll match you with an opportunity that fits your availability and skills, then schedule a brief orientation before your first activity.

Yes — we welcome corporate partnerships of every size. We offer program sponsorships, payroll giving setups, employee volunteering days, and CSR co-branding. Email partnerships@rsmc.org.pk to start a conversation.

Transparency & Governance

Yes. RSMC is registered under the Societies Registration Act 1860 in Punjab. Our financials are audited annually by an independent chartered accountancy firm, and reports are published openly each year.

Our latest report (2025) is available on the News page or by request at info@rsmc.org.pk.
