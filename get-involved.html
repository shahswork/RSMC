Volunteer

Give what you can — your time matters most.

We welcome volunteers from every walk of life. Whether you have one Saturday a month or one hour a week, here's where you can help:

  • Field volunteers — ration distribution, medical camps, school events
  • Teaching volunteers — tutoring at our learning centres (Bhakkar)
  • Skills-based — design, accounting, social media, IT, photography
  • Fundraising hosts — organize a dinner, walk-a-thon or workplace giving drive

Volunteers complete a brief orientation and basic safeguarding training before joining the field.
