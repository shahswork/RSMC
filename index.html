Our Mission

Dignity, opportunity and hope — for every Pakistani family.

Founded in memory of Rukhsana Shafqat — a mother, teacher and tireless advocate for the poor — RSMC carries forward her belief that no child should be hungry, illiterate, or invisible.

From the alleys of Lahore to the deserts of Cholistan, we work hand-in-hand with local communities to deliver high-impact programs in education, healthcare, food security and women's livelihoods — built on transparency, compassion, and measurable results.

What We Do

Four programs. One promise.

Every initiative is designed for measurable, lasting change — never charity for its own sake.

Education

Scholarships, school supplies, and after-school tutoring for under-served children — with a focus on girls.

See program

Healthcare

Free medical camps, maternal-care kits and a network of community health workers in rural districts.

See program

Food Relief

Monthly ration packs and seasonal Ramadan & Eid drives reaching the families most affected by inflation.

See program

Women's Livelihoods

Vocational training, sewing machines and micro-grants to help women launch home-based businesses.
