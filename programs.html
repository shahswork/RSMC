Our Programs

Four interconnected programs designed for lasting impact — not short-term relief.

Home › Programs
Program 01

Education for every child

We believe a single educated girl can transform a generation. Our education program targets out-of-school children in low-income areas — with a special focus on girls.

  • Full scholarships covering tuition, books, uniforms and exam fees
  • After-school tutoring in Math, English and Science (Grades 1–10)
  • Two community learning centres in Lahore and Multan
  • Parent literacy workshops for mothers of enrolled children

Reach in 2025: 1,200+ children · 100 new scholarships · 96% retention rate

Program 02

Healthcare at the doorstep

For families miles from the nearest clinic — and rupees away from being able to afford one — we bring quality care to where it's needed.

  • Monthly free medical camps in under-served villages
  • Maternal & newborn care kits for expectant mothers
  • Community health-worker network trained in first aid, hygiene & nutrition
  • Emergency medical assistance fund for life-threatening cases

Reach in 2025: 38 villages · 9,400 consultations · 320-patient single-day camp record

Program 03

Food on every table

Inflation in Pakistan has pushed millions to choose between meals and medicine. We're working to make sure no family has to choose.

  • Monthly ration packs: flour, rice, lentils, oil, sugar, tea, salt
  • Ramadan & Eid drives: festive food packs and iftar meals
  • Winter blankets & food distribution in the coldest months
  • Emergency response during floods and natural disasters

Reach in 2025: 1,200 families/month · 14,400 monthly meals served

Feed a family
Program 04

Women's livelihoods

A woman with a skill — and a sewing machine — can change the trajectory of an entire family. We make sure she has both.

  • 6-month vocational training in tailoring, embroidery and beauty services
  • Sewing-machine grants at graduation
  • Basic financial literacy and small-business mentorship
  • Marketplace access to sell goods through our co-op

Reach in 2025: 4 training centres · 140 graduates · 88% income-positive after 12 months

Empower a woman
