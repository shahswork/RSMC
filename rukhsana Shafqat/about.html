Our Story

From a mother's kitchen to a nationwide mission.

RSMC was founded in 2014 in memory of Rukhsana Shafqat — a beloved school teacher and mother who, for over three decades, quietly fed, clothed and tutored neighborhood children from her own home in Lahore.

After her passing, her family and former students vowed to keep her work alive — formalising it as a registered non-profit. Today, RSMC operates across Punjab and southern Sindh, reaching over 24,500 individuals annually through four flagship programs.

We remain a small, lean organization on purpose: 91 paise of every rupee donated reach our beneficiaries directly.

Mission

Why we exist

To restore dignity, opportunity and hope to Pakistan's most vulnerable families through high-impact programs in education, health, food security and women's empowerment.

Vision

The future we seek

A Pakistan where no child sleeps hungry, no girl is denied an education, and no woman is trapped in poverty by circumstance.

Values

How we work

Transparency in every rupee. Compassion in every interaction. Excellence in every program. Community at the centre of every decision.
